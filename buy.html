Get the Palate Deck

The Kickstarter funding campaign launches early October, which will be your first chance to buy the Palate Deck. Sign up for a one-time notification when it happens.

  • First Edition

    The 104-card Kickstarter edition. With everything you need to play basic-level and slightly more advanced rounds, this is the edition to use when you're getting started. Includes Level I and Level II decks, available February 2017.

    Contents

    • 4 guide cards
    • 5 beer color cards
    • 5 intensity cards
    • 15 character cards
    • 15 family cards
    • 60 tasting cards

  • Second Edition

    When you're ready for more of a challenge, the 108-card second edition offers more varied evaluation and a ton of extra tasting cards. This edition must be paired with an existing copy of the First Edition to work. Includes Level III and Level IV decks, available June 2017.

    Contents

    • 4 guide cards
    • 6 beer color cards
    • 12 character cards
    • 76 tasting cards
    June 2017

  • Both Editions

    The full 212-card set that includes Levels I through IV from the first two editions. Available June 2017.
